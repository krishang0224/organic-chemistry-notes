## Preview
**Alcohols:**
- Physical properties (H-bonding, boiling points)
- Acidity: 1° > 2° > 3° (for alkoxide stability... wait, actually opposite — covered in detail)
- Reactions: oxidation, dehydration, esterification, Lucas test
- Victor Meyer test for 1°, 2°, 3°

**Phenols:**
- Acidic nature (pKa ~10), more acidic than alcohols
- Resonance explanation for acidity
- Reactions: EAS (ring reactions), esterification, Reimer-Tiemann, Kolbe reaction

**Ethers:**
- Physical properties
- Cleavage by HI
- Reactions (relatively unreactive)
