## COMEDK Pattern (Know This)
- **Total:** 180 questions — 60 Physics, 60 Chemistry, 60 Maths
- **Duration:** 3 hours
- **Marking:** +1 correct, 0 wrong (NO negative marking!)
- **Level:** JEE Mains level. Harder than VIT. Numerical and conceptual both.
- **Key:** Attempt ALL questions — no negative marking = never leave blank.

## Exam Day Strategy

### Time Allocation (3 hours = 180 min)
- Chemistry: 55 min (roughly 55s per question)
- Physics: 55 min
- Maths: 55 min
- Buffer for revisiting: 15 min

### Question Approach
1. **Read carefully** — COMEDK questions have tricky negatives ("which is NOT...")
2. **Attempt first round:** mark questions you're unsure about
3. **Never skip blank** — no negative marking, guess intelligently if stuck
4. **Numerical:** write formula first, then substitute — avoid silly errors
5. **Named reactions:** if you know the reagent → product, mark and move

### Don't Panic Points
- If Maths is hard, Chemistry organic saves marks
- Organic Chemistry = most consistent scoring subject
- Long calculation? Try to eliminate options with rough estimation
