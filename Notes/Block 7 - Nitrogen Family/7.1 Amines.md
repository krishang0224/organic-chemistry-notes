# 7.1 Amines

> [!warning] Note
> This note will be filled in detail when we reach Block 7.

## Preview
- Classification: 1°, 2°, 3° amines; quaternary ammonium salts
- Physical properties: lower bp than alcohols (N-H weaker H-bond than O-H)
- Basicity order: aliphatic vs aromatic amines, 1° vs 2° vs 3°
- Preparation: from haloalkanes, amides (Hofmann degradation), reduction
- Reactions: alkylation, acylation, reaction with HNO₂ (Hinsberg test)
- Diazonium salts from 1° aromatic amines → Sandmeyer reactions
- Coupling reactions
